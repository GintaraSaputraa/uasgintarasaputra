Hunter X Hunter

TV Series 2011 - 2014 16+ 24m Animation, Action, Adventure

Hunter x Hunter" is one of the most popular and beloved anime series worldwide. Created by Yoshihiro Togashi, the anime follows the journey of a young boy named Gon Freecss, who aspires to become a Hunter like his long-lost father in order to find him. Alongside his newfound friends Killua Zoldyck, Kurapika, and Leorio Paradinight, Gon ventures into a world full of wonders, dangers , and unexpected adventures. With its thrilling plot, complex characters, and epic battles, "Hunter x Hunter" has captivated millions of viewers with its exceptional quality.
